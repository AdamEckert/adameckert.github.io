Overview

DreadStorm is a very small, simple game made for an unofficial game jam among members of my game development club.
Made during the COVID pandemic and my Senior year of college, this game is a mood piece designed to express the general sense of anxiety I was feeling at the time. I am very proud of the dread mechanic, which I feel conveys the central emotion quite well, along with the layers of visual effects, floaty, turbulent movement, and sound design. While this game doesn't do much, I love how it comes together enough that I have to include it on this site.
