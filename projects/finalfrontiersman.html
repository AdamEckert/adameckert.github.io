The Final Frontiersman

Solo project (GM48, 48 hours)
Side-scrolling wave-based arcade game
GM48 Page | Itch.io Page

Overview

The Final Frontiersman was made for the 34th GM48 game jam, with "Small World" as the theme. You play as a space cowboy up against the hostile life on three different tiny planets, each one with their own little twist!
This project is perhaps my favorite to date, featuring simple and satisfying gameplay, a unique style of presentation, and mechanically distinct enemies that push the base mechanics of the game to their limit.

And, of course, I think it's pretty funny too!

Lessons Learned

When things get rough, persevere! At about two-thirds of the way through the jam, I had not made as much progress as I had hoped, and felt so dejected that I considered dropping of the jam entirely. However, I resolved to work extra hard for just a bit longer, and soon found myself coming down the other side of the hump.
