Card Defenders

Four person project (~8.5 months)
Isometric tower-defense card game
Itch.io page

Overview

As the lead designer of Card Defenders, I was in charge of documenting the behavior for all the game elements (enemies, towers, abilities), documenting the core game loop and menu/game flow diagram, designing levels, and playtesting/implementing balance changes.

Lessons Learned

Be open and honest with your team.
At times during this project, insecurities about my work and process loomed in my mind and caused me a lot of anxiety. This was resolved when I eventually decided to talk about it with another team member, who helped me reframe my thoughts to feel less troubling. This experience helped me realize that self-awareness and openness can be important to developing and maintaining a positive relationship with your work and those you work with.
