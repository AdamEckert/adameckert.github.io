Itch.io page

Overview

While I had been developing as a hobbyist for years, Kuri was my first project to have a full, formal development cycle. Made as a project in my Senior year of high school, I learned to apply basic SCRUM concepts by setting project milestones and defining tasks for each sprint in order to fit my production schedule to the class deadlines.
